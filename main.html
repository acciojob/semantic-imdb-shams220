Summary

"Inception" is a science-fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets by entering people's dreams. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can perform an impossible task—implanting an idea into someone’s mind.

Actors

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb

  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
